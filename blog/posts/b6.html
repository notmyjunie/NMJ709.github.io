The Dreamed

Last night I dreamt of something.

It's hard to put into words, so I'll do the best I could to describe what it was like.

Though the only moments I could remember was the last few ones, but they were the blissful moments I've ever had dreamt.

As I sat defeated on the floor in what seemed like a dorm room, the door opened to someone who I initially assumed to be my roommate. She(assuming based on the fact that she had the same build as me) lied down on a mattress while rambling about life being inanely repetitive.

I didn't have any clarity in that moment. The only thing that could spring into my mind on that dream was, that, we embraced each other.

I felt her warm embrace, it felt real. She called out to me, she comforted me.
The world is fucked, she whispered to my ears.
She disappears.
I weep.

She was me. And I was her, giving her support to myself.

Is this what it truly means by loving yourself? Then I shall once more return to a clean slate.
